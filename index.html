Saptapadi

On 1st step they will promise for household keeping, pure diet and to avoid those things that might harm them;

On 2nd step they will promise to develop their physical, mental and spiritual power;

On 3rd step they will promise to increase their wealth by righteous and proper means;

On 4th step they will promise to acquire knowledge, wealth, happiness and harmony by mutual love, respect and trust;

On 5th step they will pray for the blessings for strong virtuous and heroic children;

On 6th step they will promise to strive for self respect and longevity;

On 7th and final step they will promise to be true companions and remain lifelong partners.

 

On completion of saptapadi they will take first step into a new life of togetherness in presence of holly fire.
